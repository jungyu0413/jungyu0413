## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the **Visual AI Lab at Yonsei University**, advised by Prof. Hae-Gon Jeon. My research focuses on building human-centered AI systems that understand and predict complex human movements and behaviors. My ultimate goal is to develop AI technologies that genuinely help people and contribute to society through meaningful, human-focused research.

- 🔭 **Current Research:** Trajectory Prediction, Crowd Behavior Modeling, Generative World Models.

## 🚀 Projects & Open Source

### 👁️ Facial Expression & Affect Analysis
* **[Expression Recognition App](https://github.com/jungyu0413/Expression_app):** A PyQt-based application for multi-face detection and expression classification from images, videos, or real-time webcams. Visualizes softmax probability distributions.
* **[Real-time Expression Recognition (Classification)](https://github.com/jungyu0413/Realtime_FER_Classification):** Lightweight real-time facial expression recognition using softmax-based classification, including preprocessing and visualization pipelines.
* **[Real-time Expression Recognition (Valence-Arousal)](https://github.com/jungyu0413/Realtime_FER_Estimation):** An extension of expression recognition to continuous affect modeling via the valence–arousal space with visual affect mapping.

### 🎥 Real-time Vision Applications
* **[Real-time Face Detector](https://github.com/jungyu0413/realtime_face_detector):** A fast and lightweight face detector based on SSD and RetinaFace, optimized for single-person detection scenarios.
* **[Real-time Eye Blink Detection](https://github.com/jungyu0413/Eye_Blink_Detection):** Behavioral analysis tool tailored for human state monitoring. Optimized for lightweight deployment on embedded systems (useful for anxiety estimation).
